Event recap

ALTS CEE in Budapest brought together over 80 luxury travel professionals for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Central Europe. With top-tier exhibitors from around the globe and carefully selected buyers from across the region – including Hungary, Poland, Romania, Bulgaria, Slovakia, Moldova, Czech Republic, Serbia, and Slovenia – the event was a dynamic mix of networking, discovery, and meaningful connections. Set against the backdrop of Budapest's vibrant charm, we explored the city's iconic sights, savored authentic local cuisine, took in breathtaking views from the Danube, and most importantly – built relationships that lead to real business.
