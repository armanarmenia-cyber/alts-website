Event overview

Our roadshow delivers premium networking experiences through exclusive boutique evening events hosted in the elegant settings of Vilnius, Riga, and Tallinn.

Featuring a carefully curated group of 15 exhibitors and up to 20 leading luxury travel agencies in each city – specialists in high-end leisure and premium incentive travel – this intimate format is designed to foster meaningful, results-oriented connections in a region full of opportunity.
