ALTS Madeira

Madeira, Portugal

Event recap

ALTS Madeira was an incredibly beautiful event, organised in cooperation with the Madeira Promotion Bureau, Savoy Palace, a member of Leading Hotels of the World, and INSIDER Luxury DMC Madeira. From discovering luxurious hotels to savoring breathtaking landscapes, our time in Madeira was filled with enriching experiences! We immersed ourselves in the vibrant culture, mastering the art of traditional Bolo do Caco and Poncha, enjoying an authentic local folklore performance, tasting Madeira wine — the oldest wine in the world, and making meaningful and lasting connections.
