ALTS UK & EU Cascais

Cascais, Portugal

Event recap

ALTS Cascais brought together 40 international luxury travel exhibitors from top destinations, alongside elite travel designers from across Europe and the UK. Our journey through Cascais featured pre-scheduled 1:1 meetings and immersive Portuguese experiences, from a golf clinic and azulejo tile painting to wine tasting and a Porto Tonic cocktail workshop. We dined in palatial elegance at Valverde Palácio de Seteais and stepped back in time with a refined dinner at the 16th-century monastery of Ritz-Carlton Penha Longa Resort — at ALTS, it's all about people, connections, and a warm, inspiring atmosphere.
