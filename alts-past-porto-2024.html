ALTS Porto

Porto, Portugal

Event recap

ALTS Porto brought together 40 international exhibitors from different corners of the globe, including destinations, hotels, resorts, DMCs, cruise lines and private jet operators, and a great selection of high-end travel designers from Portugal, France, Scandinavia, Italy, Spain, Switzerland and the UK. Our journey through Porto included 1:1 pre-scheduled meetings and a variety of unique activities, including port wine tasting at Graham's Port, an art walk at Serralves Museum of Contemporary Art featuring the exclusive Yayoi Kusama exhibition, olive oil workshop and tasting, a vintage car tour, and a scenic yacht cruise along the Douro River.
