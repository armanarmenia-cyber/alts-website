ALTS UK & Europe Douro Valley

Douro Valley, Portugal

Event recap

We gathered in the enchanting Douro Valley, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most beautiful wine regions in the world. Nestled along the Douro River, the area is renowned for its luxurious Quintas, breathtaking views, and as the only place in the world where port wine can be made. The event connected luxury hospitality brands with premium travel designers from across Europe. Buyers from the UK and Europe – including Spain, Portugal, Italy, France, Scandinavia, Belgium, Switzerland, and others – came together for 2.5 days of networking and enjoyment among like-minded professionals.
