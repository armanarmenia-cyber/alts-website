Buyer registration form.

Are you a Premium Travel Designer, Travel Agency or Concierge company? Apply for the ALTS events and tap into the world of new business opportunities! The participation for buyers is free of charge.

To participate, we respectfully request that you meet the following conditions:
  • Operate in outbound travel markets across at least two continents;
  • Represent one of the buyer's markets which we target for the edition you apply to;
  • Provide proof of recent or upcoming bookings within the luxury travel segment;
  • Submit three references, two of which must involve direct bookings with luxury accommodations;
  • Have a minimum of 6 months' experience as a recognized professional in the industry;
  • Not use this opportunity to market or sell your product at our event.
